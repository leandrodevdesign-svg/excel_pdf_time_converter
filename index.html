Internal Workspace

A simple place to review hours, invoices, and comparisons.

This site is for internal operational work. It helps the team review Kimai or Excel exports, convert invoice PDFs into a structured CSV, and compare worked hours against invoiced hours before sending information to clients.

Batch

Typical Flow

A practical order for the most common work.

01

Start with the source file

If you only need to inspect one export, open Single. If you need several files or several users, open Batch.

02

Review hours and records

Check totals, daily load, projects, activities, usernames, and detailed records before preparing anything for comparison or export.

03

Generate invoice CSVs when needed

If your starting point is a set of invoice PDFs, open Invoices to Excel and convert them into a clean CSV first.

04

Compare Kimai and invoices

Open Kimai vs Invoices when you need to check whether invoiced hours match the worked hours exported from Kimai.

05

Use Client Overview for summaries

After reviewing Batch data, use Client Overview when you need a cleaner client-facing summary of the workload.

06

Export or download the result

Depending on the page, finish by exporting a PDF report or downloading the generated CSV for the next step in your process.
